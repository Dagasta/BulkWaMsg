# Get Free Redis from Upstash - 2 Minute Setup

## Step 1: Go to Upstash
Open this link: https://upstash.com/

## Step 2: Sign Up (Free)
- Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"**
- Sign up with **GitHub** or **Google** (fastest)
- No credit card needed!

## Step 3: Create Redis Database
1. After logging in, click **"Create Database"**
2. **Name**: `bulkwamsg`
3. **Type**: Select **"Regional"** (free tier)
4. **Region**: Choose closest to you (any region is fine)
5. Click **"Create"**

## Step 4: Get Connection String
1. Your database will be created
2. You'll see a page with connection details
3. Look for **"Redis URL"** or **"Connection String"**
4. It looks like: `redis://default:xxxxx@xxxxx.upstash.io:6379`
5. **Copy the entire URL**
